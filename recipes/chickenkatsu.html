Chicken Katsu

Description

This is the best chicken dish ever

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves.
  • All-purpose flour helps seal in the moisture, adds flavor, and promotes browning
  • Salt & pepper
  • 1 egg
  • neutral oil with a high smoke point, such as canola or vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Season the chicken, then dredge in flour.
  2. Coat each breast in egg, then press into the Panko.
  3. Fry the chicken katsu until golden brown.
